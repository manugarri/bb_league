About Blood Bowl League Tracker

What is Blood Bowl?

Blood Bowl is a fantasy football board game created by Games Workshop. It combines American football with the fantasy races of the Warhammer universe, resulting in a brutal, tactical, and hilarious game where Orcs, Elves, Humans, and other races clash on the gridiron.

About This App

The Blood Bowl League Tracker is designed to help coaches and commissioners manage their leagues and tournaments. Key features include:

  • Team Management - Create and manage your teams with full roster tracking
  • Player Progression - Track SPP, skills, and injuries
  • League Organization - Set up leagues with custom rules and formats
  • Schedule Generation - Automatic round-robin scheduling
  • Match Recording - Detailed match result tracking
  • Statistics - Comprehensive stats for players and teams
